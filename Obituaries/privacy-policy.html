4. Use of cookies

4.1 What are cookies?

Like most websites, this Website uses cookies and similar technology. A cookie is a small amount of data, which often includes a unique identifier that is sent to your computer or mobile phone (referred to here as a "device") browser from a website's computer and is stored on your device. Each website can send its own cookie to your browser if your browser's preferences allow it, but (to protect your privacy) your browser only permits a website to access the cookies it has already sent to you, not the cookies sent to you by other websites. Many websites do this whenever a user visits their website in order to track online traffic flows.

On this website cookies are used for anonymous measurement of which pages users visit. Users have the opportunity to set their devices to accept all cookies, to notify them when a cookie is issued, or not to receive cookies at any time. Each browser is different, so check the "Help" menu of your browser to learn how to change your cookie preferences.

During the course of any visit to the Funeral Zone website, the pages you see, along with a cookie, are downloaded to your device. Many websites do this, because cookies enable website publishers to do useful things like find out whether the device (and probably its user) has visited the website before. This is done on a repeat visit by checking to see, and finding, the cookie left there on the last visit.

4.2 How does the Funeral Zone website use cookies?

Information supplied by cookies can help us to analyse the profile of Funeral Zone’s visitors and help us to provide you with a better user experience.

Funeral Zone also uses various independent measurement and research companies who gather anonymous information regarding the visitors to Funeral Zone’s website on Funeral Zone’s behalf using cookies, log file data and code which is embedded on Funeral Zone’s website. We use this type of information to help improve the services we provide to Funeral Zone’s users. All third parties are strictly required not to use any information for their own business or other purposes.

4.3 Third Party cookies on Funeral Zone pages

Please note that during your visits to the Funeral Zone website you may notice some cookies that are not related to Funeral Zone. When you visit a page with content embedded from, for example, YouTube or Vimeo, you may be presented with cookies from these websites. Funeral Zone does not control the dissemination of these cookies. You should check the third party websites for more information about these. If you register with us or if you continue to use the Website, you agree to Funeral Zone’s use of cookies.
