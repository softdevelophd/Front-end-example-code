ABOUT FUNERAL ZONE

Funeral Zone is a free online resource that helps and supports the bereaved.

The site features online obituaries, funeral director listings and funeral-related information.

Funeral Zone is a partner of the NAFD

Funeral Zone Obituaries

A Funeral Zone obituary brings the community together, providing a safe place for family and friends to pay their final respects to a loved one. The obituary allows the bereaved to create a legacy for their loved one, as well as give friends and family easy access to practical details such as the location and time of the funeral service.

Visitors can leave condolence messages on the obituary, light candles and donate to charity in memory of their loved one. Funeral Zone obituaries are FREE. They are created by funeral directors for their bereaved clients as a complimentary service to help them through their time of grief.

Funeral Organiser

funeral zone organiser The Funeral Organiser contains a series of how-to guides to help the bereaved. Articles in the Funeral Organiser include How to Register a Death, How to Write a Eulogy, A Guide to Funeral Costs, A Guide to Green Funerals and How to Choose a Funeral Director.

There is a funeral hymns section where the bereaved can browse through a library of the most popular hymns sung at funerals, such as Abide With Me and All Things Bright And Beautiful. There is also a funeral poems section containing the most popular funeral poems read out at funerals, such as Goodbye by Catherine Turner and Memories by John Greenleaf Whittier.

Funeral Director Listings

Choosing the right funeral director is an important task for the bereaved and their families. Funeral Zone provides a directory of local funeral directors for the bereaved to browse through and research about.

By clicking on a funeral director’s profile page the bereaved can read about the funeral director, access their contact details, locate the funeral home and make a direct enquiry to them.

The bereaved can also view Funeral Zone obituaries that the funeral director has created for their clients, giving an indication of the level of service they provide.
